The Great Spring Tip Off

Frequently Asked Questions

The Great Spring Tip Off is an Annual Competition where players can tip horses throughout the Spring Carnival. It's like putting $1 each way on a horse, so whether it wins or places, you will earn points. The player with the most points at the end of the competition wins.

After logging in, navigate to the Tip page, select your race, choose a horse, and submit your tip before the tipping window closes.

Yes, you change your tips as much as you like until the tipping window closes (5 minutes before race start). If your tip is scratched after tipping, you will recieve the Official TAB Subsitiute for that race.

You can add the site to your home screen on mobile devices. On iOS, tap the share icon and select "Add to Home Screen". On Android, use the browser menu to add it directly.

A joker is a X2 multiplier that can be applied to ANY race. You get three to use throughout the competiton so choose wisely!

Our results will be according to the official results on the TAB website, if a protest is upheld, that will be applied to the tipping results.

Entry is $30 (plus processing fees)

Contact the Mock Sports as soon as possible via their social media channels.

While unlikely, if there is a tie, the winner is the tipper with more 1st place tips.

The competition spans the 12 weeks of the Spring Carnival. Beginning with Saturday races on August 23rd.

We aim for all Saturday meetings to be released by the Thursday evening prior.
