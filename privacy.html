Privacy Policy

The Great Spring Tip-Off – The Mock Sports

At The Mock Sports, we are committed to protecting your privacy and the personal information you share with us when entering The Great Spring Tip-Off competition.

What We Collect

When you sign up for the competition, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Residential Address

This information is used solely for the purposes of competition management, communication, and eligibility verification.

How We Store Your Information

All personal data is securely stored in encrypted files behind multiple protective firewalls and access controls. Only authorised personnel involved in running the competition have access to these files.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal details with any third parties.

In the Event of a Breach

If there is any suspected or confirmed unauthorised access to your personal data, you will be notified as soon as possible. We will take immediate steps to investigate the breach and limit any potential damage.
